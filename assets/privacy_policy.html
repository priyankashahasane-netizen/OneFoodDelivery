Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our delivery driver application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide to us when you register and use the App, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, address, vehicle information
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID, driver's license number, vehicle registration details
  • Payment Information: Bank account details, payment method information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through the App's chat feature, customer support interactions

1.2 Location Information

We collect precise location data when you:

  • Enable location services in the App
  • Go online to receive delivery requests
  • Accept and complete delivery orders
  • Navigate to pickup and delivery locations

Location data is collected continuously while you are online and during active deliveries. You can disable location services, but this may limit your ability to receive delivery requests.

1.3 Device Information

We automatically collect information about your device, including:

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers (UDID, device ID)
  • Mobile network information
  • IP address
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data

1.4 Usage Information

We collect information about how you use the App, including:

  • Orders accepted, completed, and canceled
  • Delivery history and earnings
  • Time spent online/offline
  • Features accessed and interactions within the App
  • Ratings and reviews received

1.5 Transaction Information

We collect information related to your delivery activities, including:

  • Order details and delivery addresses
  • Payment transactions (cash and digital payments)
  • Earnings and disbursement history
  • Cash in hand balances

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your driver account
  • Match you with nearby delivery opportunities
  • Facilitate communication between you, customers, and restaurants
  • Process payments and calculate earnings
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send important notifications about orders, payments, and account updates

2.2 Safety and Security

We use your information to:

  • Verify your identity and eligibility to use the platform
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and illegal activities
  • Ensure compliance with our Terms and Conditions
  • Investigate and resolve disputes
  • Enhance the safety and security of our services

2.3 Service Improvement

We use your information to:

  • Analyze usage patterns and improve App functionality
  • Develop new features and services
  • Conduct research and analytics
  • Personalize your experience
  • Optimize delivery routes and efficiency

2.4 Communication

We use your contact information to:

  • Send you order notifications and updates
  • Communicate about your account, earnings, and payments
  • Send important service announcements
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent, where required)

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Customers and Restaurants

We share limited information necessary for delivery services:

  • Name and profile photo (visible to customers when order is assigned)
  • Real-time location (visible during active deliveries)
  • Phone number (for delivery communication)
  • Vehicle information and estimated arrival time

3.2 With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who help us operate the App, including:

  • Payment processors for payment transactions
  • Cloud storage and hosting providers
  • Analytics and crash reporting services
  • Communication and messaging services
  • Map and navigation services
  • Customer support platforms

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Legal processes, court orders, or government requests
  • Enforcement of our Terms and Conditions
  • Protection of our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigation of potential violations or fraud
  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information with other parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

4. Location Data

Location data is critical for our delivery services. We collect and use location information to:

  • Match you with nearby delivery opportunities
  • Provide real-time tracking to customers
  • Optimize delivery routes
  • Ensure timely deliveries
  • Enhance safety and security

Location Tracking Modes:

  • Foreground: When the App is open and active
  • Background: When the App is running in the background (required for receiving order requests while online)

You can control location permissions through your device settings. However, disabling background location may prevent you from receiving delivery requests.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive information is stored in encrypted databases
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Authentication: Secure login and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Payment Security: Payment information is processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain business records

Retention Periods:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for a period after account closure
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • Location Data: Retained for operational purposes and deleted after a reasonable period
  • Communication Records: Retained for customer support and dispute resolution

You may request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request a copy of your data

You can update most information directly in the App's profile settings.

7.2 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and personal information through:

  • The "Delete Account" feature in the App
  • Contacting customer support

Note: Some information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

7.3 Location Permissions

You can control location permissions through:

  • Device settings (iOS/Android)
  • App settings within the App

Changing location permissions may affect your ability to use certain features.

7.4 Communication Preferences

You can manage notification preferences in the App settings. However, you cannot opt out of essential service-related communications (order notifications, payment updates, etc.).

7.5 Data Portability

You may request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format. Contact customer support to make such requests.

7.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on your consent to process personal information, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time. This may affect your ability to use certain features.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze App usage and performance
  • Improve security and prevent fraud
  • Provide personalized experiences

You can control cookie preferences through your device settings, though this may affect App functionality.

11. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the App
  • Notifying you through the App or email
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

We do not sell your personal information. To exercise your California privacy rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification (correction)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

To exercise your GDPR rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Some browsers and devices support "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. Currently, we do not respond to DNT signals because there is no industry standard for doing so. We continue to monitor industry developments regarding DNT technology.

16. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may affect your personal information, we will:

  • Take appropriate remedial actions
  • Report to relevant authorities where required

  • Receiving notifications and communications as described
